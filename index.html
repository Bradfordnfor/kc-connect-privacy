Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-20

This privacy policy applies to the KC Connect app, created by Nfor Ringdah Bradford.

What We Collect

When you register and use KC Connect, we collect:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Profile photo (if uploaded)
  • Role and institution details (student, alumni, or staff)
  • Bio, career information, and mentorship preferences (alumni only)
  • Messages and files shared in chat rooms
  • Study resources and documents you upload
  • Event registrations
  • Payment information processed through Campay (mobile money transactions)
  • Subscription status and payment history
  • Device IP address and basic usage data (pages visited, time spent, operating system)

We do not collect your precise geographic location.

Artificial Intelligence

KC Connect includes an AI-powered educational assistant powered by Google Gemini. Messages you send to the assistant are processed by Google's Gemini API and are limited to educational content only. See Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles that data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, file storage, and real-time chat
  • Campay — mobile money payment processing (MTN and Orange Money)
  • Resend — transactional emails (OTPs and notifications)
  • Google Gemini — AI educational assistant

We do not sell your personal information to any third party. We may share your information only when required by law, to protect user safety, or with the service providers listed above who are bound by confidentiality obligations.

Your Data, Your Rights

  • You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing kcconnectnoreply@gmail.com
  • You can stop using and uninstall the app at any time
  • We will respond to data deletion requests within a reasonable time

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, data may be retained for a limited period for legal or fraud-prevention purposes.

Children

KC Connect is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has registered, contact us at kcconnectnoreply@gmail.com.

Security

Passwords are never stored in plaintext. Authentication and data storage are handled securely through Supabase with encrypted credentials.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy. Check this page periodically for updates.
